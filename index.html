CUSTOMS BROKERAGE


John M. Brining, Co., Inc. is a Mobile, Alabama-based customs broker with a pristine national reputation coupled with strong family values.

In 2016, they became a subsidiary of Dunavant Logistics Group, adding in-house brokerage to the Dunavant portfolio of customer service offerings.

Whatever the unique circumstances of your business, we will clear your shipment through customs and all other necessary government agencies, meeting your individual needs and carefully following your specific instructions. Because you interact with only one individual when we manage your customs brokerage requirements, your shipment status is always readily available.



Cargo Consolidation and Charter Brokerage
